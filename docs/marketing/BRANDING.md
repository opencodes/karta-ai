# 🏛️ Karta: Brand Identity

## 1. The Name
- **Core Name:** Karta (कर्ता)
- **Meaning:** "The Doer" / "The Agent" / "The Master of Action."
- **Domain:** [karta.ai.in](https://karta.ai.in/)
- **Positioning:** Moving away from a passive "Assistant" to an active "Agent."

## 2. Mission Statement
> To empower individuals by transforming passive data into active intelligence across finance, time, and relationships.

## 3. Brand Voice & Personality
- **Tone:** Professional, Concise, Proactive, and Trustworthy.
- **Persona:** A high-level Digital Chief of Staff. 
- **Language Style:** Hybrid (Modern Tech + subtle Sanskrit roots).
- **Key Phrase:** "Karta doesn't just record; Karta executes."

## 4. Target Audience
- **Power Users:** People managing multiple income streams.
- **Networkers:** Professionals with 500+ active contacts.
- **The Organized:** Individuals who want a "Single Source of Truth" for their life.
